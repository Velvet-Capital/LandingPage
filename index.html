3: Select the portfolio you wish to use

DeFi, or Decentralized Finance, refers to the use of blockchain technology to create decentralized financial systems and services that operate independently of traditional financial intermediaries. This includes things like decentralized exchanges (DEXs), lending and borrowing platforms, and more. The goal of DeFi is to create a more open, transparent, and accessible financial system that is not controlled by a small group of centralized institutions.
